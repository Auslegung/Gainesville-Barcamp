Sponsor Gainesville Barcamp 2016!

Sponsor Gainesville’s un-conference! A tech-focused eventwhere anyone can get involved!

Sponsoring a BarCamp is a great way to support and give back to the Gainesville tech and creative community. The conference features one day of talks and presentations from enthusiasts and professionals. By sponsoring the event, you’ll have the opportunity to expose your company to around 200 local creatives and technologists, as well as participate in the success of a much-loved event.
